About the system

We want to support the work for you as supplier, manufacturers or marketing managers for methods in Norway. All methods to be used in specialist health will have been through a technology assessment either at national or local level. This is part of the main goals of the national system for the introduction of new methods in specialist services.

A key part of the implementation of methodological assessment consists of obtaining proper documentation of the method. This is where you as supplier and/or manufaturer will have an important role, with access to significant documentation and knowledge. The National System aims to ensure that patients in Norway are treated with safe, secure, and cost effective methods. To achieve this goal, the system is dependent on close cooperation between all participants involved.
